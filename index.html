Our Vision

To be the guardians of the Andaman sea while sharing its magic. We believe in sustainable tourism—showing you the beauty of the reefs today, ensuring they remain vibrant for generations to come.

About Us

More Than Just
A Travel Agency

We are a collective of island natives, marine biologists, and PADI-certified professionals. Unlike generic aggregators, we live here, dive here, and know every hidden cove of Havelock.

Our History

What started in 2014 as a humble two-man team with a single wooden boat has grown into Havelock's most trusted adventure provider. Over the last decade, we have safely guided over 15,000+ travelers into the deep blue, evolving from simple guides to curators of luxury island experiences.
