Know Before You Go

Internet Connectivity

Internet is slow on the island. Only BSNL and Airtel have decent coverage. Download maps offline before arriving.

ATMs & Cash

ATMs frequently run out of cash. Please carry sufficient cash. We accept UPI but connectivity issues can occur.

Ferry Timings

Last ferry from Port Blair is usually at 2:00 PM. Ensure your flight lands well before 12:00 PM.
