Scuba Diving (Shore Dive)

3 Hours Ages 10+ Moderate Intensity

Overview

Experience the thrill of breathing underwater. This program is designed for beginners (non-swimmers) and includes a training session followed by a guided dive at Nemo Reef.

Itinerary

  • Pickup from hotel (if selected).
  • Training on breathing techniques and hand signals (20 mins).
  • Gear up and enter shallow water.
  • Underwater dive with instructor (45 mins).
  • Photos and videos transfer.

What to Wear/Carry

  • Swimwear or comfortable t-shirt/shorts.
  • Towel and change of clothes.
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses.
  • Pendrive/Phone for photos.
